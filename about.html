Our Mission

Understanding Architecture Impact

Multi-agent LLM frameworks are widely adopted, but their architectural impact on performance remains poorly understood. We aim to bridge this gap by providing systematic evaluation and actionable insights.

Enabling Better Design

Our research translates empirical findings into concrete architectural design principles, helping engineers and builders create more efficient, scalable multi-agent systems.

Open Science

MAFBench is open source, enabling the community to evaluate frameworks, contribute benchmarks, and advance the state of multi-agent AI systems together.
